LPU LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS - COMPLETE GUIDE

=== HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE ===

STEP 1: Login to UMS Portal
- Visit: https://ums.lpu.in
- Enter your Student ID and Password
- Click "Student Login"

STEP 2: Navigate to Leave Section
- Go to "Student Dashboard"
- Click on "Leave Application" or "Apply for Leave"
- Select leave type

STEP 3: Fill Leave Application Form
Required Information:
- Leave Type (Medical/Personal/Emergency/Family Function)
- Start Date
- End Date
- Number of Days
- Reason for Leave (detailed)
- Contact Number during leave
- Emergency Contact

STEP 4: Upload Supporting Documents
- Medical Leave: Doctor's certificate mandatory
- Emergency: Relevant proof
- Family Function: Invitation/proof (if required)

STEP 5: Submit Application
- Review all details
- Click "Submit"
- Note down application number

STEP 6: Approval Process
- Application goes to Class Coordinator
- Then to HOD
- Final approval from Dean
- Email notification sent at each stage

=== LEAVE TYPES ===

1. MEDICAL LEAVE
- Requires: Doctor's certificate
- Maximum: 7 days at a time
- Approval: Usually within 24 hours

2. PERSONAL LEAVE
- Requires: Valid reason
- Maximum: 3 days per month
- Approval: 2-3 days

3. EMERGENCY LEAVE
- Requires: Emergency proof
- Can be applied retrospectively
- Approval: Priority basis

4. FAMILY FUNCTION
- Requires: Advance application (7 days)
- Maximum: 5 days
- Approval: Subject to attendance

=== IMPORTANT RULES ===

Attendance Requirement:
- Minimum 75% attendance mandatory
- Leave affects attendance percentage
- Medical leave may be exempted (with certificate)

Application Timeline:
- Apply at least 2 days in advance (except emergency)
- Retrospective leave: Apply within 3 days of return
- Late applications may be rejected

Maximum Leave:
- Total leave per semester: 15 days
- Continuous leave: Maximum 10 days
- Frequent leave may affect academics

=== LEAVE APPROVAL AUTHORITY ===

For 1-2 days:
- Class Coordinator approval sufficient

For 3-5 days:
- HOD approval required

For 6+ days:
- Dean approval mandatory

=== LEAVE CANCELLATION ===

How to Cancel:
- Login to UMS Portal
- Go to "My Applications"
- Select leave application
- Click "Cancel Request"
- Provide reason for cancellation

Cancellation Rules:
- Can cancel before approval
- After approval: Contact coordinator
- Partial cancellation not allowed

=== EMERGENCY LEAVE PROCEDURE ===

If unable to apply online:
1. Call university: +91-1824-517000
2. Inform Class Coordinator
3. Email to: coordinator@lpu.co.in
4. Apply online as soon as possible
5. Submit documents within 3 days of return

=== LEAVE BALANCE CHECK ===

Check Remaining Leave:
- Login to UMS Portal
- Go to "Leave History"
- View "Leave Balance"
- Shows: Applied, Approved, Rejected, Remaining

=== COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS ===

Q: Leave application not submitting?
A: Check all mandatory fields, ensure documents uploaded

Q: Leave rejected?
A: Contact coordinator for reason, reapply with corrections

Q: Attendance below 75% after leave?
A: Apply for attendance condonation with valid proof

Q: Emergency leave without documents?
A: Submit undertaking, provide documents within 7 days
